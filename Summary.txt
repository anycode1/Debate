Summary

cultural values - cv
modern values - mv

Equality:
cv have rigid hierarchies(caste, gender, age).
mv have equality & meritocracy

Individual Freedom:
cv prioritizes family/majority decisions i.e. arrange marriage, career choices.
mv people have autonomy for decisions

Science:
cv completely defy science(astrology & superstitions) and have pointless rituals.
mv focus on science, progress in med, tech, etc

Gender equality:
cv places women in inferior roles in almost all cultures(sati, no education, no opportunity, etc)
mv like feminism empower women

Innovation:
cv focus on past knowledge and preservation of old
mv focus on creativity, questioning, and reform

mv don't completely reject cv, but provide much better, positive framework for society and hence more imp
